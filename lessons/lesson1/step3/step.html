Operations on Characters and Strings

You can use predefined functions on chars. Like getting the ASCII code:

Char.code 'a'


Or converting a character to its equivalent uppercase character:

Char.uppercase 'c'


You can use predefined functions on strings. Like getting the length of a string

String.length "John"


Or something more fun like concatenate a list of string by adding a separator:

String.concat "-" ["a"; "b"; "c"; "d"]
